POLLUTION

Air pollution would be a thing of the past as there would be no cars on the road, no boilers would be allowed and other things that cause air pollution would be abolished if possible. Everyone would have a special light-metre which would measure the amount of light their house gave off at night. There would be fines if you gave off too much light. This way people wouldn't have their lights on as much at night. Noise pollution also wouldn't happen because of the reasons mentioned above.
