ENERGY

Like we said, all homes would have solar panels but this wouldn’t be enough to power the whole city. Therefore we will put solar panels on top of every roof that is exposed to sunlight so we can generate electricity efficiently. Wind turbines will also be stationed on the outskirts of the city to generate a little bit more energy.

 A system called plant-e is being developed currently and this generates energy from plants. We would also attach this to people’s balconies so they can generate even more electricity.
