HOMES

Every home would be a spacious, airy flat. Everyone would be able to decorate their home as needed. Every flat would also have a balcony to grow fruit and vegetables, would be south facing to maximise the sunlight and would have 2 solar panels to generate some of the energy for their house.

To make sure that everyone could have a home, these flats would be 3D printed cheaply. Not only is this low-cost, it is also fast to build. I have researched 3D printing and have found that the materials used are renewable.

Rainwater harvesting would be used to flush your toilet system. Rainwater harvesting is when you capture rainwater via gutters, pipes and other ways so that it can be used in and around your house.
