TRANSPORT

Everyone would travel by bike, walking or for a longer journey, a superfast magnetic railway line. This would hover in the air using magnets, so there is absolutely no friction and therefore less energy is used. These trains would run on electricity and would be made of old cars, as in 2120 all cars will be illegal in the city, no matter whether they are electric or not.
